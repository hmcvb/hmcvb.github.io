About Us

Harvard Men's Club Volleyball was started in 2009 by two Harvard undergraduates, Will Eger '12 and Beau Tremitiere '11.


The club is comprised of students from across the different schools at Harvard University including the College, Law School, Graduate School of Arts and Sciences and Kennedy School.


We practice three times a week at the Malkin Athletic Center and play bi-monthly tournaments in the Yankee League. Our weekend practice is open to everyone, while the two weekday practices are for active team members.
